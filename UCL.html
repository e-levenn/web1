The UCL

Group A

In group A, Bayern Munich lead eight points clear of Copenhagen. Both Man United and Galatasaray will not be making it to the knock-out stage of the competition.

Group B

In Group B, Arsenal lead four points clear of PSV, despite having lost their last game. Both Lens and Sevilla will not be making it to the knock-out stage.

Group C

Real Madrid dominated, eight points clear of Napoli after impressively winning all five of their last games. In Group C, Braga and Union Berlin will not be making it to the knock-out stage.

Group D

Real Sociedad and Inter Milan emerged victorious in Group D after holding a tie at twelve points. Benfica and RB Salzburg will therefore not be making it to the knock-out stage.

Group E

Atletico Madrid finished four points clear of Lazio to qualify for the knock-out stage. Feyenoord and Celtic wre, however, not as lukcy as A.Madrid and Lazio.

Group F

Borussia Dortmund finished three points clear of Paris Saint-Germain allowing both teams to qualify for the knock-out stage.Ac Milan and Newcastle United will not be taking part in the knock-out stage.

Group G

Manchester City held an impressive record of five back-to-back victories from their last five games. RB Leipzig finished second to earn itself a spot in the knock-out stage. Both Young Boys and Crevena Zvezda were not as lucky as to qualify to the knock-out stage.

Group H

Barcelona emerged victorious in Group H after bagging six points to finish three poiints ahead of Fc Porto and Shaktar Donetsk who garnered three points each. Antwerp will therefore not be participating in the knock-out stage since they finished fourth.
